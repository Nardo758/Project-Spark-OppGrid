Your agent is overcomplicating this by conflating two concepts:
Concept 1: "Included in tier" (which reports are formally part of a subscription tier's benefits)
Concept 2: "Has credits to spend" (whether user can generate without paying right now)
For OppGrid's model, you don't need Concept 1 at all. Here's why:
Your tiers give credit allocations, not report-type restrictions. A Starter user with 1 credit can use it on a Deep Clone or a Feasibility Study — their choice. The credit is fungible.
The agent is trying to preserve is_report_included_for_tier() which checks if included_in_tier="pro" applies to a starter user. That's the wrong question. The right question is just: does this user have credits remaining?
