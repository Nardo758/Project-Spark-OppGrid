What OppGrid Is:
An AI-powered opportunity discovery & business intelligence platform that helps users:

1. Discover market opportunities (problems to solve)
2. Validate business ideas with AI analysis
3. Research markets with real data (FRED, BLS, SEC, Census)
4. Generate professional reports (27 report types)
5. Connect with experts and leads marketplace
