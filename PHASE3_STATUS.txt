================================================================================
                          KEY RESPONSE FIELDS (NEW)
================================================================================

Every opportunity now includes:

success_probability (0-100)
  → "How likely is this to succeed RIGHT NOW?"
  → Combines AI score + momentum + risk + market fit
  → Example: 85 (85% likely to succeed)

confidence_interval (0-100)
  → "How sure are we?"
  → Higher validation count = higher confidence
  → Recent data = higher confidence
  → Example: 90 (90% confident in our prediction)

data_freshness_hours (int)
  → "How old is the data?"
  → Markets change fast; prefer recent data
  → Example: 12 (data is 12 hours old)

trend_momentum (string)
  → "Is this trend accelerating or slowing?"
  → Detects momentum shift automatically
  → Example: "accelerating" (speeding up)

momentum_metrics (dict)
  → acceleration_factor: 1.45 (45% faster than baseline)
  → direction: "accelerating"
  → 7/30/90-day growth rates

market_health (dict)
  → market_health_score: 0-100 (100 = hot)
  → saturation_level: emerging/growing/mature/saturated
  → demand_vs_supply: bullish/neutral/bearish
  → business_count: 45 (number of competitors)

risk_profile (dict)
  → overall_risk_score: 0-100 (0 = safe, 100 = critical)
  → saturation_risk: Too many competitors?
  → trend_fatigue_risk: Is demand declining?
  → seasonal_risk: Is this seasonal business?
  → execution_risk: How hard to execute?

reasoning (string)
  → Plain English explanation
  → "Base score: 75 + Momentum: +10 - Risk: 5 + Market: +5 = Success: 85"
